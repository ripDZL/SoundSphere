SoundSphere Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

SoundSphere does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect or process any personal information. All audio control and settings take place locally within your browser.

Data Sharing

No data is ever transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

Permissions

The permissions requested by SoundSphere (tabs, activeTab, host permissions, and storage) are used solely to enable audio control and user preference saving. No data from these permissions is collected or sent outside of the user's device.
